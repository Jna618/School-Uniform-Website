How to Measure Your Child

1
Chest: Measure around the fullest part of the chest, keeping the tape level and snug but not tight.
2
Height: Have your child stand straight against a wall, barefoot. Mark the top of the head and measure to the floor.
3
Waist: Measure around the natural waist, one inch above the belly button. Keep one finger between tape and body.
4
Between sizes? We recommend sizing up, especially for Husky fit. Growing kids need room!

💡 Pro Care Tips
  • Turn inside out before washing to preserve color vibrancy
  • Treat stains immediately with cold water — hot water sets stains
  • Remove from dryer promptly to minimize wrinkles
  • Avoid fabric softeners — they break down the stain-release treatment
  • Wash with similar colors to maintain brightness
